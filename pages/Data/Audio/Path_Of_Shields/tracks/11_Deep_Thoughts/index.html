In the depths of our being, thoughts reside,
A dance of complexity, a wild, turbulent tide.
They whisper and shout, in relentless array,
Intense and profound, within simplicity they sway.
Thoughts, like echoes, reverberate within,
Unveiling secrets, a labyrinth to begin.
They bloom like flowers, fragile and true,
Yet hold universes, vast and imbued.
Within their simplicity, lies infinite depth,
A cascade of notions, both whispered and kept.
From the mundane to the profound,
Thoughts navigate worlds, both lost and found.
In their intensity, emotions combine,
Love and sorrow, joy and decline.
They paint our world, in hues unseen,
A vibrant mosaic, emotions unforeseen.
Through simplicity, thoughts find their voice,
Unveiling truths, offering us choice.
In their profound simplicity, wisdom gleams,
Guiding us through life's bewildering streams.
So cherish each thought, both simple and deep,
For in their whispers, our soul's secrets keep.
In simplicity's embrace, they find their way,
Intense and profound, in every thought we say.
