In the world where shadows dwell and secrets hide,
The Hermit roams, with wisdom as his guide.
Eyes piercing through the veil of murky night,
He seeks the truth, with unwavering sight.

Persons of the dance of darkness and the flickering light,
The Hermit wanders, driven by insight.
He treads a path that few dare to pursue,
Unearthing hidden truths, both old and new.

With lantern in hand, a beacon in the abyss,
He illuminates the mysteries that persist.
Through labyrinthine passages, he roams,
Seeking solace in the quiet of his own.

The Hermit's gaze, an unyielding stare,
Pierces through illusions, laying truths bare.
Within the depths of his introspective soul,
He finds the essence of self, his truest goal.

In the duality of Justice and Devil's reign,
The Hermit seeks harmony, a balance to maintain.
He understands the shadows that reside within,
Yet embraces the light, letting his soul begin.

Through trials and tribulations, he finds his way,
Navigating the worlds of night and day.
With steady steps, he charts his own course,
Guided by intuition, his inner voice the source.

In solitude, he finds strength to endure,
Embracing the mysteries that remain obscure.
Eyes fixed upon the truth, he walks with grace,
In the depths of his being, he finds his rightful place.

So, in the gaze of the Hermit, worlds collide,
Justice and Devil coexist, side by side.
Through introspection and unwavering gaze,
He unites their essence, in harmonious ways.

In the world of the Hermit, secrets unfurl,
A journey of self-discovery, a precious pearl.
With eyes on you, he sees beyond the mask,
Embracing the shadows, completing life's task.
