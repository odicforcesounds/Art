Life, the grand theater of choices untold,
Where wisdom guides the paths we behold.
In every moment, a crossroad appears,
And in our hearts, a whisper of fears.
A party woven with threads of chance,
Each decision a dance, a cosmic stance.
Like a puzzle, choices interweave,
A labyrinth of paths, hard to perceive.
Some choices sparkle, enticing and bright,
Like stars in the sky on a clear moonlit night.
They beckon us forward with promises grand,
But often lead to shores of shifting sand.
Others lurk in shadows, tempting and sly,
Masking their true nature, ready to defy.
They seduce with illusions of easy gain,
Yet leave us in sorrow, drowning in pain.
Wise choices are not always clear,
They may require courage to face our fear.
They call upon intuition, a guiding light,
To discern what is true, what feels right.
A wise choice may not be the easiest route,
But it carries a depth, a wisdom to tout.
It seeks the path of growth and inner grace,
A choice that leaves a lasting embrace.
For life is a party of choices we make,
A delicate balance, give and take.
In each moment, we carve our own way,
Shaping our destiny, day by day.
So let us pause and listen to our soul's voice,
To make choices that make our hearts rejoice.
With wisdom as our compass, we navigate,
Embracing life's journey, our fate.
