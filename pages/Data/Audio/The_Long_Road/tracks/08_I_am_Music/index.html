In every beat, I weave a golden shower.

I am the rhythm that knows no divide,
Uniting hearts, casting hate aside.
Notes like flowers, bloom in the air,
My symphony, a testament to love's affair.

Through strings and keys, I play my part,
Igniting compassion in every heart.
Lyrics as weapons, to fight for peace,
Melodies that heal, never to cease.

For in the world of sound and tune,
A revolution echoes, stirring the moon.
I am Music, the catalyst of change,
Shaking foundations, rearranging the range.

Oh, nations torn by war and strife,
Hear my plea, embrace the fife.
Lay down your arms, let music guide,
In its harmonies, true peace resides.

For the real bomb, in truth, is me,
In melodies that set your spirit free.
Let music be your weapon of choice,
And fill the world with a harmonious voice.

So, let the symphony of love resound,
And in its cadence, let peace be found.
I am Music, the perfect bomb,
Exploding hearts with a peaceful psalm.
