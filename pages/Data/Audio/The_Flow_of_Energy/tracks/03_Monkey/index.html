In ancient tales of Japan, a story is told,
Of the Three Wise Monkeys, wise and bold.
See No Evil, Hear No Evil, Speak No Evil they say,
Guiding us through life, in their own special way.
The first monkey, with hands upon its eyes,
See No Evil, it whispers with a sigh.
It teaches us to turn away from darkness and strife,
To focus on goodness and the beauty of life.
The second monkey, with hands by its ears,
Hear No Evil, its message crystal clear.
It reminds us to filter out negativity's sound,
And listen to the whispers of peace all around.
The third monkey, with hands on its mouth,
Speak No Evil, it proclaims from the south.
It guides us to be mindful of the words we convey,
To spread kindness and love, in every word we say.
Together, these monkeys form a sacred trio,
With wisdom and guidance that eternally flow.
Their teachings, profound, in their simplicity,
Remind us of values held universally.
For in a world filled with chaos and noise,
The Three Wise Monkeys offer their poise.
They show us the path to a virtuous way,
Guiding our steps, day after day.
In their presence, we find solace and peace,
Their teachings, like a gentle breeze.
So let us embody their wisdom and grace,
As we navigate life's intricate maze.
See No Evil, Hear No Evil, Speak No Evil, they preach,
A lesson of harmony, within our reach.
Embrace their teachings, with an open heart,
And let their wisdom be a guiding art.
