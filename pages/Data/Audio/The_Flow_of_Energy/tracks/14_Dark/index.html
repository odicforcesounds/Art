Dark Flow's journey through the cosmic space.

Dark Flow's path, a mystical maze.
Its direction veiled, its purpose for sure,
Into the darkness, it will lure.
No promises of stars to guide,
No mercy shown on this cosmic tide.

In dark embrace, they ebb and flow.
A cosmic ballet, a somber trance,
Dark Flow's, no mortal chance.
To fathom depths of its allure,
Within its grip, we are obscure.

If fate should beckon you to peer,
Into the depths of the unknown,
Beware the Dark Flow's silent at home.
For in its currents, darkness reigns,
Untouched by joy or gains.
In mystic dance, it carries on,
The enigma of the cosmic yawn.
