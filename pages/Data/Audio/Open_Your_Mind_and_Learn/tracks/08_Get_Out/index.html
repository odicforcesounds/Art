In the depths of a soul longing to be free,
Lies the agony of one trapped, longing to flee.
A spirit burdened, yearning to escape the same place,
Seeking solace in a new horizon, a different space.
The suffering lingers like a heavy weight,
Binding the heart, sealing the fate.
Each day a struggle, a relentless fight,
Against the chains that bind, obscuring the light.
The walls close in, stifling the air,
An unyielding grip, suffocating despair.
The same place becomes a prison of the mind,
Confining the spirit, leaving no respite to find.
The longing for change, a persistent ache,
Fueling the fire, igniting the escape.
Dreams of new beginnings, of distant shores,
Whispered promises of liberation, hope restores.
The heart yearns for freedom, to spread its wings,
To soar above limitations, where freedom sings.
The shackles that bind, they shall be broken,
As the spirit awakens, no longer unspoken.
In the darkness of despair, a glimmer of light,
Guiding the way through the never-ending night.
Courage emerges, a flame within the soul,
Igniting the will to leave, to finally be whole.
For those who suffer and seek to break free,
Know that the journey begins with a plea.
Summon the strength, summon the might,
Embrace the unknown, embrace the flight.
For the soul that longs to leave the same place,
May it find liberation, and a newfound space.
May suffering be transformed into strength and grace,
As the spirit finds solace, in a new embrace.
