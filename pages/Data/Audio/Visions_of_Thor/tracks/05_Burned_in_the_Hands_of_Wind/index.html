Letter of Love that express the resume of full dream.


Which include, reality!(previsions)



My dearest Buddha, embodiment of compassion,
In the vastness of your love, I find my salvation.
Your teachings, like a gentle breeze, soothe my weary soul,
Guiding me towards enlightenment, making me whole.
But in the depths of my devotion, I stand perplexed,
For my nature, oh Shiva, is fiery and complex.
The winds of passion swirl within my being,
Threatening to consume, to leave me unseeing.
In the stillness of your presence, I hear your plea,
"Out of compassion for me, don't destroy my gifts," you decree.
And so, I grapple with this paradox, this divine dance,
Balancing the flames of my essence, taking a chance.
For I am the one burned in the hands of wind,
Caught between the flames and the silence, twinned.
In the tempest of my existence, there lies a truth,
A cacophony of noise, a silent melody, uncouth.
But in this chaos, I find solace and release,
Embracing the contradictions, finding inner peace.
For it is within the dance of opposites, my dear,
That the symphony of existence becomes clear.
In the noisy silence, I surrender to the flow,
Embracing the impermanence, letting it all go.
For in the hands of wind, I find liberation's call,
Ablaze with passion, yet surrendering to the fall.
So, my beloved Buddha, with love I write,
Navigating this path, through darkness and light.
In this eternal dance, where opposites collide,
I find the truth of my being, with you by my side.
Burned in the hands of wind, amidst noisy silence,
I embrace the paradox, the divine violence.
For it is in the fusion of elements, my love,
That I transcend, and soar to worlds above.
With profound devotion and unwavering grace,
I seek enlightenment, in this sacred space.
In the dance of our souls, let our spirits combine,
As we journey together, beyond space and time.
